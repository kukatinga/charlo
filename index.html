About Charles

With years of experience and a diverse skill set, I provide reliable and professional services to clients in Utawala and throughout Nairobi.

My journey began as a self-taught mechanic, and through dedication and continuous learning, I've expanded my expertise to include electrical work, gym equipment repair, and professional driving.

Having lived in Utawala for many years, I've developed strong relationships with local landowners and community members, enabling me to offer unique property sourcing services alongside my technical skills.

I take pride in delivering quality work, building trust with my clients, and providing solutions that meet their specific needs.
